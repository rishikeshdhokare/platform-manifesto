# ✅ Compliance Automation Selection Guide

![Status: Reference](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Reference-blue) ![Owner: Architecture/Platform Engineering](https://img.shields.io/badge/Owner-Architecture%2FPlatform%20Engineering-purple) ![Updated: 2026](https://img.shields.io/badge/Updated-2026-green)

## 🎯 1. Overview

Compliance automation platforms continuously monitor your infrastructure, policies, and processes against regulatory frameworks - SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI DSS. They replace manual evidence collection with automated control monitoring and audit-ready reporting.

For most growing companies, SOC 2 Type II is the first compliance milestone. These platforms dramatically reduce the time, cost, and engineering effort required to achieve and maintain certification.

## 📊 2. Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Framework coverage | High | Number of supported compliance frameworks |
| Cloud integration | High | AWS, GCP, and Azure infrastructure monitoring |
| Evidence automation | High | Percentage of controls with automated evidence |
| Policy templates | Medium | Pre-built policy library and customization |
| Access review | Medium | Automated user access review workflows |
| Vendor management | Medium | Third-party risk assessment and tracking |
| Auditor experience | Medium | Auditor portal and evidence sharing workflow |
| Pricing model | Medium | Flat-rate versus per-framework pricing |

## 🧭 4. Decision Framework

Select based on your framework requirements and budget:

- **Vanta** - Best overall platform with the deepest integration ecosystem
  - Choose when you need the widest cloud and SaaS integration coverage
  - Strongest automated evidence collection reduces engineering involvement
  - Premium pricing justified by reduced audit preparation effort
- **Drata** - Best for organizations needing strong custom control mapping
  - Choose when you have complex compliance requirements across multiple frameworks
  - Excellent autopilot features and customizable control frameworks
- **Secureframe** - Best mid-range option with comprehensive framework support
  - Choose when you want strong compliance coverage at a moderate price point
  - Good balance of automation and cost for mid-size organizations
- **Sprinto** - Best budget-friendly option for startups pursuing first SOC 2
  - Choose when cost is a primary constraint and you need core compliance automation
  - Growing feature set with competitive pricing for early-stage companies

## 📏 5. Recommendation by Scale

| Scale | Recommendation | Notes |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Startup (< 50 eng) | Sprinto or Vanta | First SOC 2 certification path |
| Growth (50-200 eng) | Vanta or Drata | Multi-framework compliance |
| Enterprise (200-1000 eng) | Vanta or Drata | Complex control environments |
| Hyperscale (1000+ eng) | Vanta + GRC platform | Enterprise governance at scale |

Start compliance automation before your first audit, not during it. Retroactive evidence collection is expensive and stressful.

Assign control ownership to specific teams and individuals. Compliance is not a security team responsibility alone - it requires engineering, HR, and operations collaboration.
